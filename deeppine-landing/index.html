What we do

AI Products

We explore messy problems—accessibility, document analysis, market signals—and write them down as reproducible methods.

  • Hybrid heuristics + ML ensembles when that wins
  • Readable briefs instead of hand‑wavy decks

SaaS Development

Assurance for auditors & partners

Security & Availability

  • Controls aligned to SOC 2 (Security, Availability); certification not claimed.
  • Principle of least privilege, SSO enforced, hardware‑backed MFA.
  • Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES‑256 where supported).
  • Regional backups, disaster recovery runbooks, quarterly restore tests.

Data & Privacy

  • Data minimization by default; purpose‑bound retention windows.
  • DPA available; subprocessors documented upon request.
  • LLM prompts scrubbed of secrets; opt‑out of provider training.
  • GDPR/CCPA‑aware workflows; subject requests via admin@deeppine.io.

Software Quality

  • Trunk‑based dev, typed CI, unit + property tests, canary releases.
  • Observability: structured logs, metrics, and trace sampling.
  • SLOs tracked; error budgets gate new feature work.

Responsible AI

  • Evaluation suites for accuracy, drift, latency, and safety filters.
  • Human‑overridable systems; transparent failure modes.
  • Documentation: model cards + data lineage notes.
